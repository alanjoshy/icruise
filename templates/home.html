About Us

The web application entitled “I-CRUISE” deals with luxurious ship management. This site provides the complete information of cruises, the trip packages, reservation, multi cuisine restaurants, meeting etc. India has many major and minor ports. Some of the main ones are Mumbai, Visakhapatnam, Cochin, Mangalore and Kolkata. India’s long and varied coastline gives it ample scope for developing its coastal tourism. There are a lot of tourists who come here for taking part in adventure water sports and to go cruising. Besides cruising and boating, you can go canoeing, sea swimming, fishing and diving. Resorts and travel agencies offer multiple packages and travel options.
